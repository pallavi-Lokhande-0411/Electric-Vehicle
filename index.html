Main Component of EV

components

Battery Pack Storing Capacity 40kwh - 100kwh

Battery Pack

Price: 1000.00 - 10000.00 INR (Approx.)

The battery pack is the heart of an EV, storing electrical energy. It usually comprises lithium-ion cells and determines the vehicle's range and performance.

Electric Motor Brushless DC Motor 3000W 60 V

Electric Motor

Price:25k-30k approx.

The electric motor is responsible for converting electrical energy from the battery into mechanical energy to drive the vehicle. It's more efficient than internal combustion engines and provides instant torque.

Power Electronics Onbord Charger AC/DC Convertor

Power Electronics

Price:$40,000 approx.

This component manages the flow of electrical energy between the battery, motor, and other systems, controlling voltage, current, and frequency.

Charging System

Charging System a3

EVs have onboard chargers that regulate the process of replenishing the battery's energy from an external power source. They include components for AC/DC conversion and charging control.

Thermal Management System

Thermal Management System

Maintains optimal operating temperatures for the battery, motor, and other crucial components to ensure efficiency and prolong their lifespan.

Regenerative Braking System:

Regenerative Braking System:

Captures and converts kinetic energy during braking into electrical energy, which is then stored back in the battery, enhancing efficiency and extending range.

Controller or Electronic Control Unit (ECU)

Controller or Electronic Control Unit (ECU)

Manages and controls various operations in the vehicle, including the motor, battery, and other subsystems, ensuring their coordination and efficiency.

Transmission x

While many EVs operate without traditional transmissions due to the direct power delivery of electric motors, some use single-speed or multi-speed transmissions for better efficiency.
